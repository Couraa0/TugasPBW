Detailed History

Strawberries have a long and fascinating history. They were first cultivated in ancient Rome and were highly prized for their medicinal properties. In the 14th century, strawberries were grown in French gardens, and by the 16th century, they were being cultivated in England. The modern strawberry, as we know it today, was developed in the 18th century by crossing two different species of wild strawberries from North America and Chile. This new hybrid strawberry was larger and more flavorful than its predecessors, and it quickly became popular in Europe and North America.

Various Uses Of Strawberries

Strawberries have numerous uses. One of that most famous uses of strawberries is being used as recipe or an ingredient in preparation of drinks, meals or snacks. Strawberries are used as ingredient in many types of food like cakes, yoghurts, desserts, salads, juices, meals and chocolates.
